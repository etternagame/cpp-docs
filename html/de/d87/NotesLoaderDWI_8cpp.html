Determine if the note in question is a 192nd note.

DWI used to use <...> to indicate 1/192nd notes; at some point, <...> was changed to indicate jumps, and `' was used for 1/192nds. So, we have to do a check to figure out what it really means. If it contains 0s, it's most likely 192nds; otherwise, it's most likely a jump. Search for a 0 before the next >:

posthe position of the step data.
Returns
