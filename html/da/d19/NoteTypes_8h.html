◆ ROWS_PER_BEAT

const int ROWS_PER_BEAT = 48

The number of rows per beat.

This is a divisor for our "fixed-point" time/beat representation. It must be evenly divisible by 2, 3, and 4, to exactly represent 8th, 12th and 16th notes.

XXX: Some other forks try to keep this flexible by putting this in the simfile. Is this a recommended course of action? -Wolfman2000
