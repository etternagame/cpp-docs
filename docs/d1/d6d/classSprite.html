◆ m_CustomPosCoords

float Sprite::m_CustomPosCoords[8]
private

Set up the coordinates for the position.

These are offsets for the quad the sprite will be drawn to. The first two are the (x, y) offsets for the top left. The remaining six are for the (x, y) coordinates for bottom left, bottom right, and top right respectively. These are offsets instead of a replacement for m_size to avoid complicating the cropping code.
